II. The 5S Pillars (12 min)

This section dives deeper into each of the 5S principles with key points and explanations.

2.1 Sort: Eliminate unnecessary chemicals, outdated PPE, clutter (safety hazards, wasted space)

  • 2.1.1 Eliminate unnecessary items in the workplace to prevent clutter and safety hazards.
  • 2.1.2 Identify and discard expired chemicals, outdated personal protective equipment (PPE), and unused tools.
  • 2.1.3 Establish clear criteria for sorting, such as frequency of use and item condition.
  • 2.1.4 Designate discard areas for unwanted items.

2.2 Set in Order: Designated storage areas, clear labeling (faster access, fewer errors)

  • 2.2.1 Organize the remaining items in a designated and clearly labeled storage location.
  • 2.2.2 Ensure easy access and retrieval of frequently used materials and equipment.
  • 2.2.3 Implement a clear labeling system for all containers and storage areas.
  • **This promotes efficiency and reduces time wasted searching for items.**

2.3 Shine: Regular cleaning, spill response procedures (safety, prevents contamination)

  • 2.3.1 Maintain a clean and sanitary work environment to improve safety and prevent contamination.
  • 2.3.2 Establish regular cleaning routines for equipment, surfaces, and common areas.
  • 2.3.3 Assign cleaning responsibilities and ensure proper spill response procedures are in place.
  • 2.3.4 **A clean environment fosters a sense of pride and promotes a culture of safety.**

2.4 Standardize: Documented procedures for maintaining 5S (consistency, minimizes risk)

  • 2.4.1 Develop and document standardized procedures for maintaining the first three S's (Sort, Set in Order, Shine).
  • 2.4.2 Create visual aids like checklists and diagrams to ensure consistent implementation across the workplace.
  • 2.4.3 Regularly review and update standardized procedures as needed.
  • 2.4.4 **Standardization promotes a culture of accountability and continuous improvement.**

2.5 Sustain: Regular audits, team involvement (maintain improvements, long-term benefits)

  • 2.5.1 Foster a culture of 5S by integrating it into daily routines and performance evaluations.
